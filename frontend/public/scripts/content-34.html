You Are the Help Until Help Arrives

Learn five simple steps that may save a life

  • Call 100
  • Stay Safe
  • Stop the Bleeding
  • Position the Injured
  • Provide Comfort

Life-threatening emergencies can happen fast and emergency responders aren’t always nearby. You may be able to save a life by taking simple actions immediately. You Are the Help Until Help Arrives.

Why get involved when someone is badly injured?

According to a recent National Academies of Science study, trauma is the leading cause of death for Indians under age 46. Life-threatening injuries require immediate action to prevent an injured person from dying. Those nearest to someone with life-threatening injuries are best positioned to provide first care.
